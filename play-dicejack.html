    I'll write the rules out better later, but for now these are the general rules:

    • - Initial roll happens automatically. 2 D6 are rolled and the target is this multiplied by 3
    • - Player 1 goes first (revolutionary) and rolls 2D6 as many times as they want until they stand, bust or hit the target (Dicejack)
    • - If player 1 doesn't bust or hit dicejack, player 2 can roll 2 D6 as much as they want to try and beat P1s score, again until they stand, bust or get dicejack
    • - If P1 stands then P2 beats them, P1 drinks the difference between both scores
    • - If P1 stands, P2 can choose to stand below P1s score and drink the difference
    • - If any player busts, they drink the amount they were over the tagret by
    • - If any player gets dicejack, the other player drinks the initial roll (target divided by 3)
    • - If both players draw, they both drink the difference between their scores and the target.
